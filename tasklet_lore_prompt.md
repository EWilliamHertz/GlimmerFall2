# Tasklet Instructions: GlimmerFall TCG Lore Generation

**Goal:** Write immersive, captivating, and professional lore for the "GlimmerFall TCG" website. The text must read like a premium fantasy/sci-fantasy Trading Card Game, designed to hook players into the universe and help them choose their favorite faction before they buy their First Edition Booster Boxes.

Please generate the following two sections:

## 1. The World of GlimmerFall (Introduction)
Write a 2-3 paragraph introduction about the universe. 
*   **Theme:** A world torn between cosmic Light (The Glimmer) and absolute Void (The Fall).
*   **The Conflict:** The Nexus—a crystalline core that holds reality together—has shattered into four distinct realms. Now, powerful "Summoners" use "Resonance" to call forth Entities and cast powerful Rites to seize control of the Nexus fragments.
*   **Tone:** Epic, mysterious, high-stakes, and cinematic.

*   **Solari (The Light):** 
    *   *Theme:* Divine radiance, angels, blinding truth, order.

    *   *Theme:* Deception, assassins, the void, forbidden knowledge.

*   **Aether (The Magic):** 
    *   *Theme:* Cosmic storms, wizards, manipulation of space and time.

## Output Formatting
Please output the final text in beautiful Markdown with clear headings (`H2` for sections, `H3` for factions). Keep the prose engaging but concise enough to be displayed on a modern web page without creating a wall of text.
